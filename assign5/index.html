The Dendrology Project

Our mission is to unlock the hidden patterns in weather and environmental systems. We believe these patterns reveal a deeper understanding of how our world functions, empowering us to make better decisions for a sustainable future.

Resonant Clarity

  • Beyond Linear Forecasting: Traditional models see weather in A leads to B fashion. We seek patterns across scales, revealing the feedback loops that drive complex behavior.
  • Data-Driven, Nature-Inspired: We draw inspiration from interconnected systems found in nature, applying those principles to massive climate and environmental datasets.
  • Collaboration at the Core: Our project welcomes scientists, technologists, and anyone who shares our vision of a world where we work in harmony with natural systems.

Our Focus Areas

Real-world applications of the work include:

  • Ultra-Accurate Weather Prediction: Foreseeing not just the next storm, but long-range trends impacting communities and infrastructure.
  • Climate Impact Mitigation: Identifying vulnerable areas AND finding the best places to invest resources for resilience.
  • Ecosystem Modeling: Understanding how climate shifts impact biodiversity, food webs, and ecosystem health as a whole.
